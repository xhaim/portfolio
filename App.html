EXPENSE TRACKER

An expense tracker is a tool or application designed to help individuals monitor and manage their spending. It enables users to record their expenses by entering the necessary details, such as the amount spent, date, and a brief description. The tracker we've created specifically focuses on tracking expenses, providing a simple and efficient solution. With this app, users can click on the input area, which opens a form for entering expense information. Once entered, the expenses are displayed in a list view, allowing users to easily view and review their spending history. This straightforward interface facilitates quick expense logging and provides users with a clear overview of their expenditures. The primary purpose of the expense tracker is to promote financial awareness and encourage responsible spending habits. By consistently inputting their expenses into the list view, users can gain insights into their spending patterns, identify areas where they may be overspending, and make informed decisions about budgeting. Additionally, the ability to view and review the list of expenses helps users track their financial progress over time. While this expense tracker focuses solely on expense tracking, it serves as a valuable tool for individuals seeking to maintain better control over their finances and achieve their financial goals.

The login/register form for the expense tracker app offers users a secure way to access their personal expense data. Existing users can log in by entering their credentials, while new users can register by providing essential information. Once registered, users will proceed to the home UI of the app, where they can start tracking their expenses. This streamlined process ensures a smooth transition from registration to the app's main interface, empowering users to take control of their financial management effortlessly.

Our expense tracker app features a user-friendly interface designed for effortless expense management. With a simple input area, users can easily enter the expense name and amount. Upon clicking the "Add Expense" button, the app instantly displays the expenses as a list view. Notably, the app automatically calculates and updates the total expense amount, providing users with real-time insights into their spending. This intuitive design ensures a seamless experience for tracking expenses and maintaining an accurate overview of their financial records.
